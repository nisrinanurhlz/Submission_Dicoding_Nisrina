Skintific

Skintific is one of the beauty products from Canada that has been laboratory-tested as the best choice for the tropical skin of Indonesians. With natural ingredients, alcohol-free, paraben-free, and SLS-free, it is safe for all skin types and can certainly be used by pregnant and breastfeeding mothers.

Beauty Of Joseon

Beauty Of Joseon is one of the beauty products from Korea and suitable for use on dry skin according to the seasons in Indonesia. This skincare product is very helpful in improving the skin barrier. It is a bit pricey, but there is no need to doubt its benefits.

Wardah Beauty

Wardah is one of the local beauty products from Indonesia. Wardah offers various types of skincare products to suit your skin's needs. For those with dry skin, one of the options is the 'Lightening' product, which not only brightens but also restores skin moisture.


Wardah has been awarded the Halal Green Beauty predicate, where Wardah products adhere to 5 philosophies: Halal, Natural, Alcohol-Free, Local and Global Expert, and Cruelty-Free.
